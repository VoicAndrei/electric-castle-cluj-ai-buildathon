# EC Recommends — Thursday (Day 1)

First full festival day. Energy is fresh, lines are short, discovery sets are at their best.

## Thursday vibe
1. **(re)ENERGIZING zone by Doppelherz** — wake-up routine.
2. **Castle Market by Mastercard** — explore inside the castle, shop, photograph.
3. **Royal Feast by Lidl** — proper meal before the night starts.
4. **Comedy Box stand-up at EC Village** — laugh now.
5. **Gerovital Must Have Gallery** — selfie corner with mirrors.
6. **Purcari Wine Area** — bliss break from beer.
7. **The Traitors by ProTV at the Castle** — interactive game.
8. **Dark Zone by Netflix** — themed lounge.
9. **Tonight's headliners** — check [/app/lineup](/app/lineup) for who's on Main, Hangar, Booha.
10. **Stables Stage at sunrise** — typically the last set of the night, around 07:00.

## Practical
- Bring water — Thursday's lineup is long.
- Bar lines start building around 22:00.
- If you're shuttling from Cluj, the last bus back is late but check the day's exact schedule.

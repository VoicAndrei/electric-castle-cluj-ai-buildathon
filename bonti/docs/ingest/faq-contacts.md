# Who to contact at Electric Castle

For routine questions, the catch-all is **contact@electriccastle.ro** or a WhatsApp message. But most EC topics have a dedicated team — route there directly when you can.

## Tickets & orders
- **General ticketing, order issues, login problems, password resets** → orders@electriccastle.ro
- **Tax invoices** → financier@electriccastle.ro or fill the form at electriccastle.ro/request-invoice
- **Small name typos / data corrections** → contact@electriccastle.ro

## Volunteering
- **All volunteer questions, applications, certificates, status checks** → volunteers@electriccastle.ro
- **Application page** (when calls are open) → electriccastle.ro/volunteers
- Calls open in spring and are announced on the website and EC's social channels.
- **If the call is closed:** write to contact@electriccastle.ro to be added to the additional list — your message gets forwarded to the Volunteer Coordinators.

## Partnerships & sponsorships
- **Collaborations, sponsorships, brand partnerships** → sponsors@electriccastle.ro
- Using the EC logo or organizing contests/giveaways with EC tickets is restricted to official partners. Verify with sponsors@electriccastle.ro before publishing anything that uses EC branding.

## Vendors
- **Food trucks / food vendors** → food@electriccastle.ro
- **Craft vendors / handmade products** → applications open through electriccastle.ro and EC social when the call is announced.
- If the call has closed for the current edition, apply again next year.

## Jobs & creative roles
- **Internships and full-time positions** → electriccastle.ro/careers
- **Working during EC (bar team, cashiers, crew)** → apply through EC's seasonal recruitment posts on social and the website.
- **Photographer / videographer team** → the team coordinator contacts applicants when openings exist; the team is usually complete.

## Accessibility (disabled access)
- **Free festival invitation for eligible persons** → fill the dedicated form linked from EC's accessibility page (supporting documents required).
- **Companion invitation:** eligible persons receive an invitation that also covers one companion — General Pass type, no camping included.
- If your supporting document is no longer valid, an updated one is needed before the invitation is issued.

## Lost & found
- **On-site (during the festival):** Info Point near Main Stage.
- **By email (anytime):** lost@electriccastle.ro
- **In-person pickup (after the festival):** Cluj, Str. Piezișa 19, Monday–Friday 10:00–18:00. Courier delivery can also be arranged via the lost@ email.

## Safety on-site
- **EC Safety Line:** +40 741 069 443
- **National emergency:** 112
- **Red Team** (red vests) — find any across the festival for harassment, lost friends, drink concerns, panic episodes.

## When in doubt
Default fallback: ask a volunteer on-site, email **contact@electriccastle.ro**, or send a WhatsApp message via the EC channel.

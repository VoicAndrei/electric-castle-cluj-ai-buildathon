# What You Can and Can't Bring

EC publishes full regulations at electriccastle.ro/terms-and-conditions. These lists are not exhaustive — security can refuse anything that looks like a safety risk.

## Allowed in the festival area
- Backpacks
- Professional cameras and video cameras
- Medications (declare at entry if relevant)
- Flags and selfie sticks
- Water in sealed plastic bottles or cans
- Sunscreen
- Hand sanitizer gel
- Portable inflatable chair

## Allowed in the camping area (everything above, plus)
- Bags and luggage
- Food and canned products (no glass containers)
- Perfumes and toiletries — glass allowed for these only
- Portable speakers (max 90 dB)

## Not allowed in the festival area
- Drugs
- Outside food and drink
- Knives, pocket knives
- Weapons or explosives (legal or not)
- Anything made of or containing glass
- Outside alcohol
- Bicycles, skateboards, scooters, hoverboards, segways
- Parasols / shades of any kind
- Hammocks
- Musical instruments
- Stickers, flyers, advertising banners
- Garden furniture
- Speakers, amplifiers, audio systems
- Drones

## Not allowed in the camping area
- Drugs
- Fire equipment, power generators, stoves, grills (camping cookers included)
- Knives, pocket knives, weapons, explosives
- Glass (except sealed perfume bottles)
- Outside alcohol
- Bicycles, skateboards, scooters, hoverboards, segways
- Parasols / shades of any kind
- Pavilions and sun parasols
- Musical instruments
- Garden furniture
- Drones

## Festival shop exception
Items bought at the festival shop are allowed in the camping area if you keep the receipt. Drinks must stay sealed.

## Body checks
Every entry includes a body check. Refusing the check means no entry — no exceptions.

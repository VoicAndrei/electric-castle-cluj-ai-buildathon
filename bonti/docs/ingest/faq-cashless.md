# Cashless & Wristband

## How payments work at EC
The festival is fully cashless. Everything is paid with your RFID wristband — no cash, no cards at bars or food vendors.

## Wristband activation
1. Exchange your ticket for a wristband at the festival entrance.
2. Log into your EC account and go to the cashless page.
3. Register your wristband and load money onto it.
4. You can also pre-register and pre-load before you arrive — the wristband links to your online account either way.

You can check your balance any time from the EC app or the cashless page. Top up online or at the on-site cashless points.

## Refund (getting unused money back)
- **Online refund (after the festival): free.** Initiate from your EC account.
- **On-site refund: 3% commission** charged.
- Online is easier and cheaper. Don't queue for it on Sunday afternoon.

## Lost wristband
Report immediately at the Info Point. Replacement requires ID matching the ticket name. Any unspent balance moves to the new wristband.

## Multi-purpose voucher status
Money on the wristband is a multi-purpose voucher (MPV) under EU rules. It's spendable at any EC vendor while the festival is live.

# Getting to Electric Castle

## To Cluj-Napoca
Cluj is the closest city. Reachable by:
- **Plane:** Cluj International Airport (CLJ), 8 km from city center.
- **Train:** regular service from Bucharest, Budapest, and other European cities.
- **Car:** A3 highway from Bucharest (~6h), or via Hungary.
- **Long-distance bus:** FlixBus, Eurolines.

## From Cluj to Bonțida — the EC shuttle
- The official EC shuttle runs from designated stops in Cluj-Napoca to the festival grounds.
- **Operating mode:** non-stop during festival days. **The bus leaves when it's full.** On the Monday after the festival, the last bus leaves Bonțida at **12:00**.
- **Duration:** ~35 minutes in normal conditions. At peak times (after big headliner sets), it can reach an hour or an hour and a half.
- **Cost:** ~15 lei round-trip.
- **Where to buy bus/train tickets:** online from EC, or from bus stations with dedicated counters. You can pay at the counter with your festival wristband or cash — there are no card terminals at the ticketing points. **You cannot buy a ticket from the bus driver.**
- **Return ticket time:** the time printed on a shuttle ticket is not strictly enforced — the ticket is valid for any one trip on the EC bus.
- **Children:** if a child occupies a seat on the bus or train, a regular ticket is needed even if their festival entry is free (under-8s).

## From Cluj to Bonțida — other ways
- **Car:** ~30 km via DN1C.
- **Motorcycle:** park in the large shared parking lot. No separate entrance fee, and there is a row dedicated to motorcycles.
- **Bike:** you can leave your bike tied to the stand near the main entrance to the festival.
- **Taxi / Uber:** works but unpredictable on peak nights.

## EC Parking Space
- **Distance to the entrance:** a 5–10 minute walk to the festival gates.
- **Pricing & access:** announced before each festival; details vary by edition.
- Different from **Car Camping**, which is a marked 3×8 m spot inside EC Village for the car + a personal tent and requires its own Car Camping ticket.
- If you might bring a car: buy the parking ticket in advance — last-minute availability is not guaranteed.

## Staying in Bonțida itself
If you're staying with a private host in Bonțida, your host handles parking authorization with the City Hall by submitting your license plates. The police enforce parking on the main road, so park where your host directs.

## Peak-night traffic
On peak headliner nights, traffic on the road back to Cluj can be extremely heavy. Plan to either stay overnight on-site, leave before the headliner ends, or wait until after 03:00 when the road clears.

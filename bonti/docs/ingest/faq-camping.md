# Camping & EC Village

## EC Village
The on-site campsite at Bonțida. Open from the Wednesday Camping Party through Monday morning checkout.

## Camping requirements
- A **Camping Pass** is required for any overnight stay on-site.
- It's only valid alongside a **4-day Festival Pass**. Day Ticket holders cannot camp.
- The camping area is adjacent to the festival area; your wristband gates both.
- You can stay any subset of nights — the pass covers the full window.

## What's good in the village
- Sunrise is part of the plan.
- No last bus to catch.
- Coffee tastes better close to the stages.
- 24/7 bonding with fellow festival-goers.
- Royal Feast and Royal Breakfast by Lidl for hot meals.
- Wonder Bar (#unlockwonder gin bar) on the campsite.

## Personal tents
- No size limits, but bring a tent appropriate for your group.
- **Awnings are not allowed.**
- Volunteers guide you to a spot — you can't pre-pick a location.

## Car Camping
- One **Car Camping ticket** per car, allowing the car + a personal tent in a marked **3×8 m space**.
- **Max 5 people per car.** Everyone needs their own festival + camping pass.
- Stay within the 3×8 markings — exceeding requires an additional Car Camping ticket.

## RV Camping
- One **RV Camping ticket** per RV, in the designated RV area.
- All occupants need their own festival + camping pass.

## Glamping & ready-to-camp
For people who like the idea of camping but want a real bed: EC Village offers glamping tents and pre-pitched options. Check electriccastle.com/ec-village for current offers — these sell out early.

## Facilities
- Showers open at any time.
- Bathrooms across the campsite and festival grounds.
- Drinking water stations.
- First-aid on-site.
- General store for last-minute supplies.

## What's not allowed
- Pets are not allowed in EC Village or on festival grounds.
- Glass bottles (except sealed perfume).
- Open fire, generators, stoves, grills.
- Weapons, drones, drugs.
- See [faq-rules](faq-rules) for the full list.

## Lockers
Available near the main entrance for valuables.

## Minors in camping
- Under 16 must be accompanied by a parent/guardian, and the parent must hold a valid camping pass.
- 16–17 with parental consent declaration may camp.

## Off-site option
If camping isn't for you, see [accommodation-cluj](accommodation-cluj) for the shuttle-from-Cluj approach.

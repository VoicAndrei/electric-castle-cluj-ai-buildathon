# Food & Drink at Electric Castle

Vendor lineups rotate edition to edition, but the categories below are stable. Most vendors have at least one vegan option — ask at the counter.

## Pizza
- **Camionetta** — Neapolitan-style.
- **Napoli Centrale** — folded pizza, photo-worthy.

## Burgers & smash
- **Furgoneta Street Chefs** — gourmet, napkin-required.
- **Meat Up** — smashed burgers.

## Meat & traditional
- **B-Owl** — bone-in.
- **D'Jamón** — Spanish ham.
- **Crispyteria** — schnitzel.
- **Cimbru** — Romanian traditional.
- **Moldo'** — Moldovan.
- **UMami** — huevos rotos with jamón.
- **Piggy Wagon** — pulled pork.
- **Smokăreală** — low-and-slow BBQ.

## Tex-Mex
- **TacoTrib** — tacos.
- **Mexicano** — spicy.
- **Pokka** — small bites.

## Vegan
- **Zero** — fully vegan menu, the dedicated stop.
- Most other vendors carry at least one vegan item.

## Asian & world
- **Wok'n Roll** — noodles.
- **Riders** — Middle Eastern.
- **IndiVan** — Indian.
- **PhoBar** — Vietnamese pho.
- **AsianLab** — pan-Asian.
- **RedBarn** — generalist.
- **ThaiMe** — Thai.

## Spanish / Mediterranean / fine
- **Patatas Bravas Point**
- **Casa España** — paella.
- **Taste of Transylvania** — fine dining, local heritage.

## Comfort & sweets
- **Fumuri** — loaded fries.
- **Churros by Pokka**
- **Clătite de Poveste** — Romanian crepes.
- **Pastel de Nata** — Portuguese custard tarts.
- **Iris Delice**, **Thaice Cream**, **Bonas Ice Cream**, **Delice Waffle**

## Camping food
- **Royal Feast by Lidl** — hot meals on the campsite.
- **Royal Breakfast by Lidl** — morning meals that don't taste like camping food.

## Drinks
- Bars stretch across the festival; lines are longest during peak headliner times.
- **Wonder Bar** (#unlockwonder gin bar), **Beer Garden**, **Purcari Wine Area**, **OMV Lounge** are all reliable.
- All payments are wristband-only — see faq-cashless.

## Castle Market
Inside the castle — shopping, local artisans, brand activations. Worth a wander between sets.

## Allergies & dietary
Ask any vendor. Most know what's in their menu; staff at Zero (vegan stop) and Royal Feast can usually point to gluten-free options.

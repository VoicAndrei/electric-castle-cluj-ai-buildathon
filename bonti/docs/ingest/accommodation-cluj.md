# Sleeping in Cluj — the "real bed" option

Cluj-Napoca is the closest city to Bonțida — 35 minutes by shuttle, ~15 lei round-trip. For people who love EC but don't want a tent, this is the standard play.

## When Cluj wins over EC Village
- You want a real bed, hot shower, and a door that locks.
- You're doing 2–3 festival days instead of 4 — commute cost stays low.
- You're traveling with a group that has mixed camping tolerance.
- You're flying in or coming on a long train ride and want a clean reset.

## What to book
- **Hotels (3–4 star):** Old city center or Mărăști. ~300–500 lei/night per room.
- **Apartments / Airbnb:** Best value for 2–4 people. Book 1+ month out — Cluj fills during EC week.
- **Hostels:** 80–150 lei/night for a dorm bed. Best for solo travelers.

Search areas: **Centru**, **Mărăști**, **Gheorgheni**. Avoid Mănăștur and Florești unless you have a car — shuttle pickup is downtown.

## Tradeoffs vs. camping
| | Cluj | EC Village |
|---|---|---|
| Bed | Real | Tent / glamping unit |
| Shower | In-room | Communal |
| Cost | Higher | Lower (pass + tent) |
| 24-hour scene | No | Yes |
| Distance to stages | 35 min shuttle | On-site |
| Late-night sets | Need shuttle timing | Walk to your tent |

## Shuttle logistics
- Runs Cluj ↔ Bonțida throughout each festival day, with extended hours through the night.
- ~15 lei round-trip, ~35 min each way.
- Buy in the EC app or at the pickup point.
- Last shuttle back to Cluj runs deep into the night — check the schedule daily, it shifts by day and by headliner ending time.

## Bonțida itself
Bonțida is a small village — limited rooms, often booked a year in advance by EC veterans. If you find something, you'll save the shuttle ride. Otherwise, Cluj is the practical answer.

## What to do in Cluj before / after
- **Centru historic** — Piața Unirii, the cathedral, café crawls.
- **Casa Tranzit / Form Space** — alternative scenes if you have a free morning.
- **Hoia-Baciu forest** — the famously haunted forest, walkable from town.
- **The Salina Turda salt mine** — 30 minutes south, worth a day trip if you're staying extra.

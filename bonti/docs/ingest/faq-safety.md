# Safety at Electric Castle

## Emergency
- For any emergency: **112** (Romanian national emergency number).
- EC Safety Line: **+40 741 069 443** — call or message for festival-specific help.
- Medical tent near Main Stage. First aid on the campsite.

## The Red Team
EC's Red Team — volunteers in red vests — are stationed across the festival grounds. They handle harassment, lost friends, drink concerns, panic episodes, and any "I don't feel safe" situation. Find one if you need help. They are trained, anonymous, and free.

## Drink safety
- Order an **"Angel Shot"** at any bar. You won't get a drink — you'll get someone who checks in with you and helps you out of a situation.
- Ask any bar for a **cap and straw** for your drink. Free, no questions.
- If you suspect your drink has been tampered with, go to the medical tent or the nearest Red Team member.

## Sexual health & consent
Visit **ginfospot.ro** for sex-related advice and resources — free, anonymous, Romanian-language friendly.

## Lost friends
- Phone signal on the campsite is weak. Agree a meeting spot in advance — the Info Point near Main is the standard reunion point.
- Red Team members can radio across the festival if you've lost someone.
- The Info Point holds anything handed in. Beer-from-the-owner is the only reward the FAQ promises.

## What to do if it rains hard
- Move to a covered stage or food area until the worst passes — EC keeps running through rain.
- The campsite has covered chill zones; don't try to wait it out inside your tent.
- Wellies > sneakers. Always.

## Quiet zones
The Wonder Bar, Beer Garden, and Beach Stage are typically the calmest spots during peak hours. EC Talks at the Castle is also a low-stimulation area.

# Tickets — Electric Castle 2026

## Ticket tiers
- **General Access Pass** — 250€ + tax. Access to all 4 festival days, all main areas. No camping included.
- **General Access Pass + Eurolife insurance** — same, with cancellation insurance.
- **Premium Access Pass** — premium-area access for all 4 days. Tier above General.
- **Ultra VIP Access** — top tier, includes hospitality areas.
- **Youth Pass U25** — discounted tier for ages 16–25. Nominal, non-transferable except via Exchange (and only to someone within the eligible age range).
- **Black Ticket** — special edition, limited release.
- **Day Ticket** — 89€ + tax per day. Single-day access, no camping eligibility.
- **Camping Pass** — required for camping. **Only valid alongside a 4-day Festival Pass.** Day Ticket holders cannot camp.
- **Car Camping** — one car + tent in a 3×8 m space. All occupants need festival + camping passes. Max 5 people per car.
- **RV Camping** — one RV in the designated area. All occupants need festival + camping passes.

## Where to buy
- Official: **electriccastle.com** / **electriccastle.ro**.
- Authorized partners: **Entertix**, **iaBilet**, **Eventim**, **billete.ro**, **Benefit**.
- 8% admin fee added at checkout.
- Avoid social media resellers (OLX, Facebook groups, etc.) — those tickets often don't work at the gate.

## Payment plan
- Split your order into 2 monthly payments — available at checkout, currently via the BT Star card program.
- If your card fails mid-installment: log into your EC account → My Orders → click **"Update credit card"** and retry, or use **"Pay remainder amount"** to settle the rest in one go.
- If the order ends up cancelled because installments stopped, the already-paid amount cannot be recovered.

## Ticket name & changes
- Every ticket is **nominal** — the name on the ticket must match your ID at the gate.
- **Free name change:** within 7 days of purchase, from your EC account → My Orders.
- **After 7 days:** changes only via the Exchange platform (15€ fee applies).
- For minor typos in the name (no transfer): email contact@electriccastle.ro — within roughly 48 hours of purchase, EC support can fix small mistakes directly.

## The Exchange platform
The only legal way to resell or transfer an EC ticket.
- Tickets and subscriptions are **non-refundable** once purchased.
- **Process:** seller generates a transfer link → buyer accesses it → buyer fills in their name and pays the **15€ processing fee** → seller presses "Send to buyer" to complete.
- EC is not a party in the transaction — verify the seller before sending money.
- The Youth Pass U25 ticket can only be transferred to another person within the 16–25 age range.

## Invitations & redeem codes
- Invitations (including Guest Passes) are **nominal and non-transferable**. They **cannot** be sold or transferred through the Exchange platform.
- **Activate an invitation / redeem code:** electriccastle.ro/redeem-access
- **Guest Pass invitations** require the mandatory 50 lei donation to Bánffy Castle to be paid before the festival ticket is generated in your EC account.
- **Lost an invitation code?** EC cannot recover it — the beneficiary is responsible for storing the code and confirmation email. The same applies to losing the email itself.

## ID at the gate
- Bring an ID that matches the name on your ticket.
- **Accepted ID documents:** national ID card (cartea de identitate / buletin), **passport**, **driving license**, or **birth certificate** — any of these with matching name/address will work.
- If your ID card is lost or expired, one of the alternatives above is enough to enter.

## Minor rules
- **Age 7 and under (including 7-year-olds):** **free** festival access, accompanied by a parent or legal guardian who holds a valid Festival Pass. The adult signs the under-16 declaration (linked from electriccastle.ro).
- **Ages 8 to 15:** child needs a paid ticket, accompanied by a parent or legal guardian.
- **Ages 16 to 17:** can attend alone with the parental consent declaration (over-16 version) filled in advance.
- **On the EC shuttle (bus/train):** even if the child has free festival entry, if they occupy a seat, a regular bus/train ticket is required.

## Lost ticket
If you forget your ticket you will not be able to enter the festival. If you lose your ticket, EC cannot issue another one.

## Age requirements
18+ for full independent access (or 16+ with the parental consent declaration). Alcohol service follows Romanian law (18+).

## Refund policy
Tickets are **non-refundable** once purchased. The only legal path to recover money is the Exchange platform.

## Downloading your ticket
- From the confirmation email after purchase.
- Or from your EC account → My Orders → PDF download.
- A valid PDF from the original confirmation works at the gate even if it doesn't appear in your EC account.

# EC Recommends — Wednesday (Arrival)

The soft open. Camping is open since Monday, but gates to the main festival open Thursday. Wednesday is for arriving, settling in, and the Camping Party.

## Wednesday vibe
1. **Get the EC app** — favorite your artists, get personal schedule alerts.
2. **Scan the food courts** — see what's on, plan tomorrow's first meal.
3. **Take a tour** — walk the festival map, get your bearings before it gets dense.
4. **Glitter at Summer Vibes by Sephora** — opening-night ritual.
5. **Reel Party by BT at Hangar** — ease into party mode.
6. **Wonder Bar at #unlockwonder** — first taste of the gin bar.
7. **Ping Pong Stage by Burn** — newer stage, check the program.
8. **Stage-hop** — twelve stages, no schedule pressure tonight. Let mood decide.

## Practical
- Camping check-in is 24/7 from Monday onward.
- Bring rain gear even if it's sunny — weather flips fast in Transylvania.
- Bar lines are short tonight; tomorrow they won't be.

Sleep early if you're doing all four days. Tomorrow starts.
